Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Thump ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the Thump app for iOS and watchOS (the "App").

The short version: All your health data stays on your device. We never see it, store it on our servers, or share it with anyone. We collect only anonymous usage analytics to improve the app.

1. Health Data We Read

With your explicit permission via Apple HealthKit, Thump may read the following health data types from your device:

  • Heart Rate -- resting, walking, and workout heart rate samples
  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV) -- SDNN measurements
  • VO2 Max -- cardio fitness estimates
  • Step Count -- daily step totals
  • Sleep Analysis -- sleep stages and duration
  • Exercise Minutes -- workout sessions and active energy

We request read-only access to these data types. Thump does not write data to HealthKit.

2. What Stays On-Device

All health data is processed and stored locally on your device. Your heart rate readings, HRV measurements, VO2 max data, step counts, and sleep information never leave your iPhone or Apple Watch. We do not operate servers that receive, process, or store your health data. All analysis, trends, and insights are computed entirely on your device.

3. What We Collect

Anonymous Usage Analytics

To improve the App, we collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage analytics, including:

  • App open events (how often the App is launched)
  • Feature usage (which screens and features are used, without any health data content)
  • App performance metrics (crash reports, load times)
  • Device type and OS version (for compatibility purposes)

We use a privacy-first analytics provider for anonymous usage analytics. No personally identifiable information is collected. These analytics cannot be tied to your identity and contain no health data whatsoever. They are used solely to understand how features are used so we can improve the App.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or personal identifiers
  • Health data of any kind
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Advertising identifiers

4. Subscriptions and Billing

Thump offers optional subscription plans managed entirely through Apple's StoreKit 2 framework. All billing, payment processing, and subscription management is handled by Apple. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information. For billing inquiries, please refer to your Apple ID account settings or Apple Support.

5. PDF Export

Thump allows you to export health summaries as PDF files. This feature is entirely user-initiated. When you export a PDF, the file is generated locally on your device and saved to a destination you choose (Files, email, messaging app, etc.). We do not receive a copy of exported PDFs or know when you create them.

6. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer your data to third parties. Because health data never leaves your device, there is no health data to share. The anonymous usage analytics we collect are used only internally to improve the App and are not shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any other external parties.

7. Data Security

Your health data benefits from the security protections built into iOS and watchOS, including device encryption, the Secure Enclave, and app sandboxing. HealthKit data is encrypted at rest on your device and protected by your device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

8. Data Deletion

Because all health data is stored locally on your device, you maintain full control over it. To delete all data associated with Thump:

  • Delete the App from your iPhone and Apple Watch. This removes all locally stored data, insights, and preferences.
  • Revoke HealthKit access via Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Thump.

No data remains on our servers after deletion because we do not store your data on our servers.

9. Children's Privacy

Thump is not designed for or directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and may notify you through the App. Your continued use of Thump after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
